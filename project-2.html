Angels

40 years had passed since a deadly mist enveloped the world rendering the outside world nearly impossible to live in. Robin and Zoe were born into this world which begs the question are they blessed with the gift of life? Or damned with the life of a rat?
After being forced to leave their safe havens, they will soon find that glowing lights in the sky and strange beings that wander the mists may have something to do with a connection that they unknowingly share.
