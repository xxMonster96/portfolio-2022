Vigilance

Throughout the city of Featherston, there have been sightings of an elusive figure that some call a hero with others calling them a villian. Whatever they are, the city is divided on whether this superbeing should either face justice or recieve praise for the work they've done for the oceanside city.

Brian Calvin is a private investigator who is hired by a prestigious political figure in Featherston to find who or what this superbeing is and bring their identity to the light.
